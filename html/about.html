Canon Concrete Contractors, Inc.

Mike and Elaine Haines established Canon Concrete Contractors, Inc. 18 years ago in Fremont County. Canon Concrete Contractors specializes in custom stamped concrete, all flatwork, monolithic slabs, concrete paving, curb & gutters, and foundations. They have completed numerous residential and commercial projects including concrete work at Holcim Inc., Old Mission Deli, Evangelical Free Church, Penrose Library, Daily Record, Estes Industries and Sonny’s Ace Home Center.

As accredited members of the Better Business Bureau, Mike and Elaine have 40 years of experience in concrete. They moved to Colorado Springs, Colorado in 1981 from Chicago, Illinois. They started their business in Colorado Springs and were involved in numerous construction projects, including Sam’s Club. In 1990 eager to raise their family in a smaller community, Mike and Elaine moved to Penrose with their daughter Kristie and son Mike.

Through their children, Mike and Elaine became very involved in the community and have been frequent supporters of youth and community programs such as the Fremont County 4-H Livestock Sale, Florence High School Wrestling and Football Programs, Penrose Apple Day, Belvedere Blue’s Festival, Orchard of Hope, Loaves and Fishes, Canon City Clock Tower and Create Canon City. Working from their home, Mike and Elaine have kept Canon Concrete Contractors a family business.

Canon Concrete provides a limited 1 year warranty. This warranty excludes ground movement, spalling (chipping and flaking) due to using de-icers and cracking caused by overweight vehicles (dump trucks, trash trucks, etc.)
