How to hire a professional contractor:

Step 1 - Research and learn

  • Find out exactly what it is you want including types of materials, stains, paints, colors, treatments, etc.

Step Two - Comparison shop

  • Call or email several different contractors in your area.
  • Be careful to pay attention to response times.

Step Three - Get a written estimate

  • It is wise to get an estimate from a number of contractors.
  • Look over the estimate carefully.
  • The estimate should include a time frame with start and finish dates.
  • A sample should be included at this point.

Step Four - Make certain the company is reputable

  • There are a lot of fly-by-night companies.
  • Make sure to check references and check with the local Better Business Bureau.

Step Five - Sign the contract

  • If there is no contract do not work with the company.
  • No verbal agreement should be done.
  • This will protect your interests as well as the company you are dealing with.
  • A written contract is the only way to do business.

Step Six - Approve the sample and pay upon completion of the job

  • Most companies will provide a guarantee of work completed.
  • Make sure to research the best company to meet your needs.

Step Seven - Enjoy!!!
